A

Adrenalin

B

Auxin

Insulin

Thyroxine

The function of ribosome in cells is

A

protein synthesis

B

starch synthesis

C

transport of material

D

lipid storage

Answer▼

Correct Answer:
**A**

The function of a ribosome in a cell is protein synthesis. Ribosomes are the sites where mRNA is translated into proteins.

A

toad

B

lizard

bird

D

cockroach

Correct Answer:

6

The villus in the small intestine is significant because

A

Increases the surface area for absorption

B

increases the surface area for digestion

C

assists in mixing digested food

D

assists in filtering undigested food

Answer▼

Correct Answer:
**A**

The villi are small, finger-like projections that line the inner surface of the small intestine. Their primary function is to increase the surface area available for the absorption of digested nutrients into the bloodstream.

9

A

crops, pests and beneficial insects

B

crops, temperature and humidity

C

pests, beneficial insects and water

D

crops, water and soil

10

Epiphytes growing on the branches of trees provide an example of the relationship known as?

A

parasitism

B

commensalism

C

saprophytism

D

holophytism
